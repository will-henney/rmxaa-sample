\begin{abstract}
  \RMxAAstart{W}elcome to the \rmaatex\ template (v1.1 31/08/2025) to prepare your contribution to the proceedings of the conference to be published in RMxAC. There are three types of macros for contributions to publication in the Serie de Conferencias of our journal:  extended, onepage, and abstract. Use examples “rm\_extended\_example”, “rm\_onepage\_example” or “rm\_abstract\_example”, depending on the case. The only abstract contribution should not exceed 300 words, including the title, author list, and addresses. This corresponds to approximately half the length of a column on the page or an abstract of about 200 words.  \\ 
This  file is an example for abstract only {\bf rmac\_abstract\_example.tex}, generally used for poster and/or short oral contributions.
\end{abstract}
